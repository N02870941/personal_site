Being Black and Getting Your Foot in the Door

Being Black and Getting Your Foot in the Door is a four part series dedicated to the professional advancement of students of color at SUNY New Paltz. On this project I am working with Dr. Cruz Caridad Bueno and Kadeen Douglas to help students develop their resumes, apply to programs such as MLT Career Prep, and take charge of their professional lives.
